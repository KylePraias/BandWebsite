Featured Bands

These bands didn't just play music — they screamed against the system, made you jump off your couch, and defined the sound of rebellion for a whole generation.

Green Day

Discover the legacy of Green Day — the band that redefined punk rock with high-energy performances and groundbreaking albums like Dookie and American Idiot. Their rise from 924 Gilman Street to global stardom lit the fuse for a new generation of punk.

  • Formed: 1987
  • Origin: East Bay, California
  • Most Iconic Track: "American Idiot"

Blink 182

Experience the fun and irreverent attitude of Blink 182, a band that mixed catchy pop-punk with a rebellious edge. Known for their wild music videos, prankster humor, and arena-filling choruses, Blink 182 made skate parks and heartbreak their stage.

  • Formed: 1992
  • Origin: Poway, California
  • Most Iconic Track: "All The Small Things"
Learn More

Rise Against

Dive into the politically charged sound and passionate lyrics of Rise Against, a band that speaks to social issues with hardcore conviction. Balancing aggressive energy with melodic emotion, they’ve made protest anthems resonate with a global fanbase.

  • Formed: 1999
  • Origin: Chicago, Illinois
  • Most Iconic Track: "Savior"
Learn More

Sum 41

Crash headfirst into the explosive fusion of punk, metal, and adolescent rebellion that defines Sum 41, a band that turned skate parks and high school angst into anthems of chaotic energy and defiant fun.

  • Formed: 1996
  • Origin: Ajax, Ontario
  • Most Iconic Track: "Fat Lip"
